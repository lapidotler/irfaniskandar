Guarana Antarctica

Product Poster Advertisement

Software: Adobe Photoshop

Date of Completion: 15/10/2022

Introduction to the Work

This project showcases a product advertisement for Guarana Antarctica, a popular canned drink in South America (SA). Originating from Brazil, the product has gained popularity not only within SA but also in places like Japan.

The project involved a creative journey, incorporating a meticulous sketching process to conceptualize and refine ideas. The sketching phase served as a crucial foundation, allowing exploration of various design elements and refining the visual direction of the project.

Final Thoughts...

As I reflect on the creative process, the combination of thoughtful sketching and the strategic implementation of a grid system proved to be instrumental in achieving a visually appealing and well-organized design. The final artwork represents the culmination of these efforts, showcasing not only the journey but also the evolution of ideas into a tangible and impactful creation.
