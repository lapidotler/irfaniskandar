Extras: Minecraft Moderator/Admin Experiences

Outside of school, I had worked as a Minecraft moderator/admin for a server called EarthPinas, which I had worked for around 2 years. This took place during the Covid period, and I felt that this experience was important to me as it helped me to gain insights into leadership, teamwork, and the dynamics of online communities.

This is something I felt that I should share, as I believe that this experience has helped me to become a better person overall. If you are interested in reading about my experiences, feel free to click the button below!

Chapter 1: Too Good to be True?

My journey as a Minecraft moderator/admin began when a friend on Discord introduced me to the a Minecraft server he was on. I was intrigued by the server's concept and decided to join it.

At the time there, I was a regular player, but an opportunity to become a staff member arised upon the server's opening of staff applications.

In the same week, I happen to not only win a Photography competition in the server, but I also did get the role of a Trial-Helper.

It didn't last long, however, as the server was shut down due to the owner's personal reasons.

However, that open up a new opportunity for me to become a staff member in another server, a server that I would soon call "My Second Home".

Chapter 2: New Beginnings

EarthPinas was a Filipino survival server where other players can play together and have fun. It later became a semi-survival server, with modded extensions & gamemodes such as PvP, PvE, Roleplay, and Towny.

Initially, the small server involved the owner, staff members and some friends. However, it soon grew to become a large community of players.

Since the shutdown of the first server, the experiences and responsibilities of the Trial-Helper role were carried over to EarthPinas.

In the 2 years of experience as a staff member, I have learned many things, such as leadership, teamwork, and patience amongst other things.

This really helped me improve when climbing the ranks become a Moderator, and eventually an Admin.

Chapter 3: Moving On

The server had slowly lose traction and popularity in 2022, and eventually, the server was shut down around 2023. Despite the server's shutdown, I still have many fond memories of the server.

I am grateful for the experiences I have gained from it, as well as the friends I have made along the way.

My time as a Minecraft moderator/admin not only enriched my gaming experience but also provided me with insights into leadership, teamwork, and the dynamics of online communities.

This experience has also taught me to be more responsible and to be more aware of my actions, which I have also carried over to my real-life experiences.
