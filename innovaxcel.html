InnovaXcel

Mobile Portfolio App Design

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD

Date of Completion: 04/08/2023

Introduction to the Work

This work showcases my potential portfolio app through the mobile landscape.

The mobile InnovaXcel app captures the interest of the audience through my portfolio gallery and the app's stunning animations.

Brand Story

At the core of my passion for app design and development lies a story of resilience, determination, and the pursuit of a dream. My journey began during my time at Republic Polytechnic, but it wasn't without its share of challenges beforehand.

In preparation for my 3rd year in secondary school, I found myself grappling with the sudden disruption caused by the global pandemic. The outbreak of Covid-19 forced students into remote learning, isolating me from the collaborative environment I was comfortable in and dampening my spirit for learning.

Tagline

"Design. Develop. Deliver."

This tagline perfectly encapsulates my brand story and the values I hold dear. As an amateur app designer and developer, I believe in the power of design and development to create meaningful experiences.

With a relentless drive for excellence, I strive to not only design and develop innovative solutions but also ensure their successful delivery to clients and users. Throughout my journey, I have faced numerous challenges and obstacles, but I have never lost sight of my goal to deliver exceptional results.

Moodboard

The Modern & Minimalistic theme signifies a brand story of simplicity, elegance, and forward-thinking. It reflects a focus on clean lines, open space, and a minimalist aesthetic, which aligns with the idea of efficiency, sophistication, and innovation.

The captivating mix of vibrant purples, pinks, and oranges in the color palette creates a modern and energetic atmosphere that embodies creativity and passion.

Color Palette

The chosen analogous color scheme brings harmony, playfulness, and a delicate feel to the brand. The smooth transition between the colors adds to the overall aesthetic appeal and contributes to the sleek and contemporary feel of the design.

The addition of a separate blue palette enhances the overall analogous scheme, bringing a calmer and eye-soothing experience for users and blending well with the mix of vibrant orange and purple.

Typography

Futura, with its clean and geometric design, brings a sense of modernity and professionalism to my brand. Nunito, on the other hand, adds a touch of elegance and readability to the overall branding.

By combining these fonts, my brand creates a unique and cohesive visual identity that reflects my dedication to delivering exceptional design and development solutions.

Logo Development

Logo 2, featuring design and development elements with a crown, was chosen to symbolize proficiency in both aspects. This logo design takes a unique turn, incorporating both the aspects of design and development as well as a crown above, portraying a skilled experience in both.
