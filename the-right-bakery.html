The Right Bakery

Online Pastry Website

Software: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD

Date of Completion: 06/06/2023

Introduction to the Work

This work portrays a brand new business venture, in this case, an online pastry website.

The Right Bakery follows the philosophy of delivering outstanding pastries at a fast and convenient pace.

Brand Story

The Right Bakery is an innovative online business in Singapore, specializing in crafting and delivering a wide array of delicious pastries. Our main goal is to offer customers a diverse selection of delectable treats.

Despite challenges like the impact of Covid-19, The Right Bakery emerges in the post-pandemic era, reimagining the way pastries are created, presented, and enjoyed. We leverage technology, adapting to the changing landscape, to provide an easy, enjoyable, and accessible experience for our customers.

Tagline

"Pastry Perfection at Your Fingertips"

"Pastry Perfection at Your Fingertips" aligns perfectly with The Right Bakery's belief and targeted goals. It emphasizes high quality, craftsmanship, and accessibility.

Moodboard

The Whimsical Delights Theme embodies the joy and whimsy of indulging in delightful pastries, symbolizing The Right Bakery's commitment to offering a magical experience.

Color Palette

Soft pastel hues of blue, pink, and purple represent the dreamy and delightful nature of the bakery's pastries. The analogous color scheme brings harmony, playfulness, and a delicate feel to the brand.

Typography

The combination of Amatic SC and Satisfy fonts brings warmth, elegance, and a handmade feel to The Right Bakery's branding. The addition of Roboto for website usage provides modernity and readability.

Logo Development

The logos, representing Modern, Vintage, and Bakery themes, showcase sleek, nostalgic, and charming designs, respectively. Each logo is crafted with specific fonts to convey the bakery's commitment to staying current, traditional craftsmanship, and inclusiveness.
