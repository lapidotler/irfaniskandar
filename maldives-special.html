Software: Adobe Illustrator

Introduction to the Work

In this project, I embarked on a creative journey that involved a meticulous sketching process to conceptualize and refine ideas. The sketching phase served as a crucial foundation, allowing me to explore various design elements and refine the visual direction of the project.

Additionally, the implementation of a sophisticated grid system played a key role in organizing and structuring the design elements. The grid system not only enhanced the visual appeal of the work but also contributed to a cohesive and well-structured final product.
